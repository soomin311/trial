Creating Surveys in Qualtrics

Project Link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uwmN54uK0FY

Overview

This screencast demonstrates the fundamentals of creating surveys using Qualtrics, a widely used platform for designing and distributing research questionnaires. The tutorial walks through the essential steps of building a simple but well-structured survey.

What the Screencast Covers

  • Logging into Qualtrics and navigating the main interface
  • Creating a new survey project
  • Adding questions using multiple formats (multiple-choice, text entry, etc.)
  • Reordering, copying, and organizing survey items
  • Customizing question options and display settings
  • Previewing the survey and testing logic
  • Basic distribution options and next steps

Reflection

This exercise gave me an opportunity to practice instructional screencasting— especially maintaining a clear pace, organizing screen movements intentionally, and narrating steps in a way that is accessible for beginners. Recording the workflow helped me recognize the importance of visual clarity, cursor emphasis, and consistent narration when demonstrating digital tools.

Using HeyGen for AI-Assisted Video Creation

Project Link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=v_iS1XSIA2Y

Overview

This screencast provides an introduction to HeyGen, a web-based AI platform for creating multimedia videos using scripts, automated narration, and customizable visual assets. It walks learners through the process of generating an entire video with minimal manual editing.

What the Screencast Covers

  • Logging into HeyGen and navigating the main dashboard
  • Entering or pasting a script for AI-generated narration
  • Selecting avatars, backgrounds, and layout templates
  • Adjusting voice style, pacing, and timing
  • Previewing the video and making improvements
  • Exporting and downloading the finalized video
  • Practical tips for producing professional and engaging AI videos

Reflection

This project helped me explore how AI video tools can streamline multimedia production. I learned how automated narration, templated visual assets, and intuitive editing features can significantly reduce the time needed to produce polished instructional content. It also helped me think critically about how to balance AI automation with thoughtful design choices.
