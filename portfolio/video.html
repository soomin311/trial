Video Production

Storytelling with multimedia, narration, and purposeful design

Microcredential Introduction Video

Project Link: https://youtu.be/cIuFKSjfqgU

Project Overview

For this project, I created a short introductory video based on my microcredential work. I began by writing scripts that explained key ideas of microcredentials, ensuring that each section had a clear message and narrative flow.

Design & Production Process

  • Scriptwriting: Wrote a structured script outlining the core concepts and purpose of the microcredential.
  • Media Curation: Collected free images and video clips from various royalty-free platforms to visually represent each script segment.
  • Visual Effects: Added zooms, pans, fade-ins, fade-outs, and other transitions to maintain viewer engagement.
  • Narration: Generated professional-sounding narration using a text-to-speech tool.
  • Audio Design: Included low-volume background music to enhance tone without overpowering narration.
  • Accessibility: Added captions for two slides to support clarity and accessibility.

Reflection

This project helped me practice end-to-end multimedia production—from scriptwriting to visual editing and audio design. By integrating images, movement, narration, and captions, I learned how to build a video that is both visually engaging and pedagogically aligned with the message I wanted to communicate.
